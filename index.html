Verdiana Grace Masanja

Hidden Figure: Historical Mathematician




Date of Birth

Verdiana Grace Masanja was born on October 12, 1954 in Bukoba (near Uganda). Not much is available online about her family; however, she is currently married and a mother of four.

Major Contribution to Mathematics & Significance

Verdiana Grace Masanja was the first Tanzanian woman to earn a doctorate in mathematics.

  1. Proponent of secondary education and mathematics education for girls and women
  2. Served as the secretary for the Commission on Women in Mathematics in Africa
  3. Served as the national coordinator of Female Education in Mathematics in Africa

Summary

Verdiana Grace Masanja is a hidden figure because very few women pursue mathematics or science-related career paths. Role models are important. Young girls need encouragement because they too can thrive in the STEM field.
